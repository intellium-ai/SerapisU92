Energetic Original (energetic_original.csv)
 - Original data provided.

Energetic Enhanced (energetic_enhanced.csv)
- Original data plus a select few molecular descriptors added by RDKit.

Energetic Original Enhanced (energetic_original_enhanced.csv)
- Original data plus all molecular descriptors from RDKit.

Energetic Only Enhanced (energetic_only_enhanced.csv)
- Only SMILES from original data used to generate all possible molecular descriptors from RDKit.

Energetic Generated Enhanced (energetic_generated_enhanced.csv)
- Additional generated data from research paper also plus select subset of molecular descriptors.
